# Sharing info at work is hard

Companies struggle to share internal success in a meaningful way that feels genuine. Internal emails from your HR or marketing department help highlight large events and milestones but don't have the same impact as someone posting on a  slack channel "hey look at the cool thing I did" and no where close to the happiness when your friend texts or calls you to tell you some good news. 

## Should we spend more time talking about the hard parts?
